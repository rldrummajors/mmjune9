Marching Spotlight!

One way to get better at Marching Band is by watching other Marching Bands that possess the "next-level" skills that we are trying to obtain! Marching Spotlight features various spectacular High School Marching Bands, College Bands, DCI groups, and possibly even WGI performances!

Year: 2015

Show Name: Inferno

Group: Carolina Crown

Location: DCI World Championships

Experience Level: DCI

Context: "Inferno" was a grandiose adaptation of Dante Alighieri’s journey through the nine circles of Hell. The show opened with Beethoven’s 9th Symphony, the eerie “Abandon all hope, ye who enter here” unveiled atop a massive black banner, with corps members vocalizing the chilling message. Crown ultimately placed 2nd in finals, with a score of 97.075.
